How it works

From sign-up to showdown in minutes

Prompt Wars is fast, social, and endlessly replayable. Every battle is a fresh test of how well you think — and how well you write.

  1. 1

    Create your fighter

    Pick an archetype, name your character, set a signature color and a battle cry that follows you into every duel.

  2. 2

    Enter the arena

    Get matched 1v1 in seconds. A shared theme is revealed to both players — you both write under the same constraint.

  3. 3

    Make your move

    Attack, defend, or finish. Choose a template or craft a custom prompt, then lock it in. No edits, no take-backs.

  4. 4

    Watch the verdict

    An AI judge scores both prompts on a transparent rubric, then a cinematic reveal crowns the winner. Climb the ranks.

FAQ

Good to know

Is Prompt Wars free to play?

Yes. Your first battle is free and the cinematic result reveal is always free. Optional credits and the Prompt Wars+ subscription unlock AI-generated video reveals and cosmetics — never gameplay advantages.

How does the AI judge decide who wins?

An LLM-as-judge scores both prompts blindly on a fixed six-point rubric — clarity, originality, specificity, theme fit, character fit and dramatic potential. Scores are length-normalized and run multiple times for stability, and you see the full breakdown and the judge's reasoning on every result. You can even appeal a ranked loss.

Is it pay-to-win?

No. There are no paid stat boosts, no paid scoring modifiers and no paid archetypes. Subscriptions and purchases only unlock AI video reveals, cosmetics and convenience. Ranked outcomes depend entirely on your writing.

Which platforms can I play on?

Prompt Wars is coming to iOS and Android. Join the waitlist below to be notified the moment it lands in the App Store and Google Play.

Is it safe? What's the age rating?

Prompt Wars is rated 18+. Every prompt is moderated before a battle resolves, AI-generated video is checked before it's shown, and every reveal carries a clear AI-content disclosure. Report and block tools are available throughout the app.
